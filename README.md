### Built by **ZeroTrust Lab**

> **AI + Cybersecurity & Privacy**  
> Safer digital experiences for students and institutions — simple, explainable, effective.

---

## 🌍 The Challenge

Educational institutions are increasingly targeted by:

- 🎣 Phishing attacks  
- 🦠 Malware distribution  
- 🔓 Identity misuse  
- 📊 Data privacy violations  

Students — especially first-year learners — often lack cybersecurity awareness.  
Institutions struggle to provide protection that is both effective and privacy-respecting.

---

## 💡 Our Solution — GuardEd-AI

**GuardEd-AI** is an AI-powered cybersecurity assistant designed specifically for educational ecosystems.

We combine:

- 🤖 Artificial Intelligence  
- 🔐 Zero Trust Security Principles  
- 🧠 Explainable Risk Modeling  
- 🛡️ Privacy-First Architecture  

To create a secure, transparent, and student-friendly digital environment.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### 1️⃣ Early-Warning Phishing & Malware Detection
- Detects suspicious links, emails, and activities
- Provides plain-language explanations
- Uses a teach-back approach to improve user awareness

### 2️⃣ Privacy-Preserving Analytics
- No raw Personally Identifiable Information (PII) exposure
- Data minimization & anonymization techniques
- Secure monitoring for classrooms and labs

### 3️⃣ Consent-First Identity & Fraud Signals
- Secure authentication flows
- Transparent consent mechanisms
- Minimal data collection approach

### 4️⃣ Explainable Security Engine
- Clear risk scoring
- Actionable recommendations
- No black-box AI decisions

### 5️⃣ Digital Hygiene Companion
Designed especially for first-year students:

- Password hygiene guidance  
- Phishing awareness tips  
- Safe browsing education  
- Real-time campus alerts


